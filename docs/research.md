## Requirement Reading

BLI Legal Tech Hackathon 2 asks builders to create legal technology around blockchain, crypto, legal, finance, compliance, AI, RWA, RegTech, and the Web3 ecosystem. Public summaries state the event is virtual, open worldwide, and has bounties/support from legal and blockchain partners. The BLI page emphasizes law, finance, compliance, mentoring, bounties, collaboration, and tools for builders.

The MVP therefore focuses on a legal/compliance audit workbench instead of a generic chatbot. It gives judges a concrete product surface:

- intake of legal and blockchain product facts
- risk triage with issue rationale
- remediation ownership across counsel, compliance, engineering, and product
- evidence hashing for audit trails
- copy-ready counsel pack for review and submission

## Audit Material Notes

The BLI resource hub links to compliance-USA, compliance-world, federal agencies, FinCEN, SEC, CFTC, custody, wallets, exchanges, Chain Abuse and fraud prevention tools, and certified cryptocurrency auditor resources. The MVP reflects that by keeping source lineage visible and generating an audit prep memo rather than claiming legal conclusions.

Past BLI-adjacent highlights from Constellation Labs emphasize verified data, digital evidence, and tamper-proof audit trails. LexProof uses deterministic SHA-256 evidence hashes to make that theme visible in the MVP without requiring a real chain deployment for the first version.

## MVP Product Thesis

Small Web3 teams often cannot tell counsel what changed, which policy backs a claim, or which facts create legal risk. LexProof AuditOS turns project facts into a reproducible review packet: a structured risk score, issue list, owner queue, evidence ledger, and memo that makes legal review faster and more accountable.

## Non-Advice Boundary

The app always labels output as audit preparation, not legal advice. BLI states it is not a law firm and supports responsible blockchain content and education. The MVP follows that boundary by generating materials for counsel review.
